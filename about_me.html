My background

I am a comparative literature major at CAS with a minor in BEMT at Stern. I transfered to complit in my second year of college, and I was previously an applied psych major. In the future, I plan to pursue a master degree in business, specifically marketing and retail science at Stern.

I was raised in CHina, Beijing specifically. When I was in second grade, I stayed in Ann Arbor, Michigan for 2 years. I enjoyed both of the vibes, as I developed interpersonal connections with friends and teachers in both places.

When I was in first grade, I started several interests that continued throughout my youngsters and even now. such as calligraphy, flute, tennis and singing. I am the founding president of the Chinese calligraphy club at CAS at NYU and I organized cross-school events with other clubs such as Artist Anonymous. I was in the school choir during elementary and high school, and was in the school orchestra during middle school. I enrolled in a Jazz band during my study away period at NYUSH.
